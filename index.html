Who We Are

Where Academic Insight
Meets Industry Reality

IDR is an industry-led training and deployment institute built for digital, cyber and technology risk roles in regulated, high-consequence environments — from financial services to critical national infrastructure. We don't just teach theory. We prepare practitioners to operate under real pressure, with real frameworks, on real projects.

Our model bridges the gap between academic rigour and professional practice. Through our UK university partnership, learners gain research-backed knowledge. Through live industry projects, they build the judgment, tools and networks needed to lead from day one. IDR graduates don't just understand risk — they manage it.
